GenAI Guide · July 2026

Generative AI Courses India 2026 — IIT, Coursera, Google & Best Options Compared

Why GenAI is the most in-demand skill in India in 2026

Generative AI has moved from experimental to production in India's corporate sector within 18 months. Every major Indian IT company (TCS, Infosys, Wipro, HCL) now has dedicated GenAI practices with hundreds of openings. Product companies (Flipkart, Zomato, Razorpay, Swiggy) are embedding GenAI into their core product stacks. Banks are deploying LLM-powered customer service, document processing and fraud detection. The demand for professionals who can build, fine-tune and deploy GenAI systems is genuinely outpacing supply.

IIT Madras — ML, GenAI & LLMs (best institutional credential)

The Advanced Certificate in ML, GenAI & LLMs from IITM Pravartak (IIT Madras's DST-funded Technology Hub) is the most technically current and institutionally credible GenAI program in India. Curriculum: transformer architecture, LLMs, fine-tuning pre-trained models, RAG (Retrieval-Augmented Generation) systems, LangChain, prompt engineering and GenAI business applications. ~₹1.5L + GST, ~6 months, live weekend sessions. NIRF #1 institution. Enroll here →

Google — GenAI for everyone (best free option)

Google's AI Essentials course on Coursera covers GenAI fundamentals, prompt engineering and using AI tools for productivity. Free to audit; ~$30 for certificate. Google also offers the Generative AI Study Jams (free, hands-on with Google Cloud's Vertex AI and Gemini APIs). For professionals wanting a basic GenAI literacy credential with Google's name, this is the fastest and most affordable route.

DeepLearning.AI — best technical GenAI curriculum

Andrew Ng's DeepLearning.AI courses on Coursera include dedicated GenAI tracks: "Generative AI with Large Language Models" (3 weeks, free to audit), "Building Systems with the ChatGPT API", "LangChain for LLM Application Development" and "Finetuning Large Language Models." Free audit / ~$200 for certificates. Best for software engineers wanting practical GenAI engineering skills.
