Free AI Courses India 2026 — NPTEL, Coursera, Google, fast.ai and More

Can you learn AI for free in India?

Yes — and the free resources available in 2026 are genuinely excellent. The gap between free content and paid programs is narrowing for learning quality. The main thing you do not get for free is a credentialled certificate from a recognised institution — but for building skills, free resources are often better than paid bootcamps.

NPTEL — best free AI content from IITs

NPTEL (nptel.ac.in) is the national platform for free IIT courses. Over 500 AI, ML and data science courses from IIT professors — the same faculty who teach in paid IIT certificate programs. Free to access, ₹1,000–3,000 for the certificate exam. Courses include: Machine Learning (IIT Madras), Deep Learning (IIT Madras), Introduction to AI (IIT Kharagpur), Python for DS (IIT Madras). The NPTEL certificate carries genuine recognition in Indian corporate hiring for entry-level roles.

DeepLearning.AI — best free AI curriculum globally

Andrew Ng's DeepLearning.AI courses on Coursera are the global standard for AI learning. ML Specialisation, Deep Learning Specialisation, LLMOps, RAG, and Prompt Engineering for Developers are all free to audit. The certificate requires a paid subscription (~$49/month). For building actual ML skills, these courses are hard to beat at any price point.

Google — free AI learning with optional certs

Google's Machine Learning Crash Course at developers.google.com is free, practical and directly applicable to TensorFlow. Google AI Essentials is a ~5-hour course with a certificate for ~$30. For cloud-specific AI, Google Cloud Skills Boost has free learning paths with optional certification exams (~$200).

Infosys Springboard — free corporate AI content

Infosys Springboard (infyspringboard.onwingspan.com) offers free AI and data science courses developed for Infosys internal training — now open to the public. High-quality content, free certificates and Infosys name recognition. Particularly useful for professionals in IT services sector.

fast.ai — best free deep learning for practitioners

fast.ai's "Practical Deep Learning for Coders" is the most respected free deep learning course for practitioners. Top-down approach — build real models first, theory later. Used by ML practitioners at top companies globally. Free at fast.ai with no certificate.

When should you pay for a certification?

Pay for a credential when you need it for job applications or promotions — specifically IIT/IIM certificates for India corporate hiring, or AWS/Azure/Google Cloud certifications for cloud AI roles. See our IIT/IIM program comparison to evaluate paid options.
