Highest-paying AI companies in India — who to target

Global tech (₹25–70L): Google India (Research/DeepMind), Microsoft India AI, Amazon Science, Meta India. FinTech unicorns (₹18–45L): Razorpay, PhonePe, CRED, Zepto, Groww AI teams. AI startups (₹15–50L): Sarvam AI, Krutrim, Murf.ai, Invideo AI, Ola Krutrim. BFSI (₹15–40L): HDFC Bank AI, ICICI Bank AI, SBI AI, Bajaj Finance. IT services (₹10–30L): Infosys AI Center, TCS AI.Cloud, Wipro AI, HCL AI. Pro tip: AI roles at FinTech and AI-native startups pay 40–80% more than equivalent roles at IT services companies.

How to negotiate an AI salary in India — practical tips

  • Anchor to market data — cite this guide or AmbitionBox/Glassdoor AI role data
  • Show projects — a GitHub portfolio with 2–3 AI projects is worth ₹2–4L in negotiation power
  • IIT certificate timing — complete the IIT certificate before applying for a new role, not after — the credential is most powerful when it's brand new
  • Target role transition not just title change — moving from "Software Engineer" to "ML Engineer" justifies a ₹4–8L jump; moving from "ML Engineer" to "GenAI Engineer" justifies another ₹3–6L
