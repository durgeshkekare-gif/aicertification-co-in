Best AI Certifications in India 2026 — IIT, IIM, Coursera & Google Compared

The AI certification landscape India 2026

India's AI certification market in 2026 has three distinct tiers: credentialed institutional programs from IITs and IIMs (highest career value in India), platform-based programs from Coursera, edX, Google and AWS (global recognition, lower India-specific value), and free-to-affordable resources like NPTEL and DeepLearning.AI (best for learning, minimal credential weight).

Choosing the right tier depends entirely on your goal — building skills, getting a credential for Indian hiring, or targeting global career paths. See our full program comparison for side-by-side details.

Top IIT AI certifications 2026

IIT Madras ML, GenAI & LLMs is the most technically current AI program in India — covering transformers, LLMs, fine-tuning, RAG and GenAI applications. NIRF #1 institution. ~₹1.5L, 6 months. Best for technical professionals building GenAI skills.

IIT Kanpur CDAO Programme is the premier AI leadership program — AI strategy, digital transformation, organisational AI governance. For senior professionals targeting Chief Digital Officer or Chief AI Officer roles. ₹2.8–4.2L, 9 months.

Top IIM AI certifications 2026

IIM Kozhikode Analytics & GenAI — no coding required, business focus, NIRF #3 management. Best for managers wanting AI skills without technical background. ~₹2L, 12 months.

IIM Indore AI & Cybersecurity — unique dual coverage of AI strategy and cybersecurity. ~₹3–4L, ~12 months. Best for IT managers and senior business leaders.

Coursera, Google and global platforms

DeepLearning.AI's ML Specialisation (Andrew Ng) on Coursera remains the global gold standard for learning ML fundamentals — free to audit, ~$200 for certificate. Google's ML Crash Course and TensorFlow Developer Certificate are essential for engineers. AWS ML Specialty and Azure AI Engineer Associate are the highest-demand cloud AI credentials in India's enterprise sector in 2026.

Free options — NPTEL

NPTEL (nptel.ac.in) from IIT consortium offers 500+ AI, ML and data science courses free. Certificate exam fee is ₹1,000–3,000. The content quality is IIT-level. For students and early-career professionals, NPTEL is the best starting point before investing in a paid certificate. Find all programs at aicertification.co.in/programs.
